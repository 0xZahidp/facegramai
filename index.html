FAQs

What is FaceGramAI?

FaceGramAI is a meme-driven project on the Ton Chain that integrates AI technology to calculate a user's Telegram age and create 3D avatars from live photos. Users can earn rewards by completing tasks, referrals, and engaging with the platform.

How do I participate in FaceGramAI?

You can participate by interacting with our Telegram Mini App bot, uploading a live photo of yourself or your pet, and completing various tasks like joining groups, following on Twitter, and referring friends. Each action will earn you points that can be redeemed for rewards.

What rewards can I earn?

You can earn $TON and other rewards by completing tasks, referring new users, and engaging with the platform. Special NFTs and points are awarded for higher levels of participation and contribution to the community.

What is the 3D avatar feature?

FaceGramAI allows users to upload a live photo, which is scanned and converted into a 3D avatar. These avatars can be traded as NFTs in the marketplace or used in various gamified interactions within the platform.

How does the referral system work?

The referral system is not linear. Users earn more points by referring people with higher fan value. The more valuable the referral, the more points you earn, creating a dynamic engagement strategy for our community.

How do I get involved in the community?

You can join our community through Telegram and other social media platforms. We have groups dedicated to discussions, updates, and events where you can interact with fellow users and stay informed about the latest developments.
